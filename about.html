About Choppies

Our story, mission and values since 1986

Oir story

Choppies Supermarket was founded in Lobatse which is a small town located in Botswana in 1986. It was first called Wayside Supermarket and later changed to Choppies Supermarket as the brand grew into one of the most recognised retail in Botswana and across southern africa.

By 2003, Choppies had already expanded opening stores throughout Botswana. Today, Choppies operates over 161 stores Southern africa with stores in South Africa, Zambia, Nambia and Zimbabwe serving thousands of customers every day

Choppies is listed on both the Botswana Stock Exchange (BSE) and the Johannesburg Stock Exchange (JSE), making it one of the most prominent retail brands in the region.
